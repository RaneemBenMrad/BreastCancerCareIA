About Us

Meet Ranim Ben Mrad

Ranim Ben Mrad is a passionate data scientist who leverages her professional skills to make a meaningful impact. With a deep commitment to women's health, Ranim created "Here For HER" to support women around the world in their fight against breast cancer. Through advanced AI tools, educational resources, and a supportive community, she aims to empower women with knowledge and hope.

Our Mission

"Here For HER" is a platform dedicated to providing women with accessible tools and information about breast cancer. From AI-driven tumor predictions to educational content and a compassionate chatbot, we strive to be a beacon of support and empowerment for women everywhere.
